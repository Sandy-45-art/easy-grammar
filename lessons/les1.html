NOUNS

What is a Noun?

A noun is a word that names a person, place, or thing.

1. Nouns for People

  • Teacher

  • Mother

  • Friend

These are people. A noun can be someone you know!

2. Nouns for Places

  • School

  • Park

  • Home

These are places. A noun can be where you go!

3. Nouns for Things

  • Apple

  • Ball

  • Car

These are things. A noun can be something you see or touch!

A person is a noun. A place is a noun. A thing is a noun. Nouns are all around you!
