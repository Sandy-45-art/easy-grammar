VERBS

What are Verbs?

Verbs are words that tell us what someone or something is doing. They are action words. Whenever you do something, you are using a verb!

Examples of Verbs

Run

When you move quickly with your legs.

Example:

The boy can run fast.

Eat

When you take food into your mouth and chew.

Example:

She likes to eat apples.

Sleep

When you close your eyes and rest

Example:

The baby is sleeping

Jump

When you push yourself off the ground with your legs.

Example:

The cat can jump high.

Read

When you look at words and understand them.

Example:

He loves to read books.

Why Do We Use Verbs?

Verbs help us describe actions. They tell us what is happening in a sentence. For example, in the sentence "The dog runs," "runs" is the verb because it tells us what the dog is doing.

Practice

Think about what you are doing right now. Are you sitting, reading, or eating? These are all verbs. Try to find the verbs in the sentences you hear or read.
